Earth Day 2015 Website

Background

Earth Day is probably the most well-known civic observance in the world. Earth Day Network (EDN) is an organization that educates the public about climate change and the importance of sustainability. For its 45th anniversary, they have launched the 2015 campaign website themed “It’s Our Turn to Lead.”
Despite its influence, the site is very basic, something that provided Earth Day 2015 with a web presence and not much more. The site is very difficult to navigate, and it's very confusing to the users as what actions to take.

Goals for the project

  1. Create a more colorful and structured interface, brings the site more in touch with its younger audience.
  2. Use clear calls to action to compel users to action.
  3. Keep the content clear and concise from beginning to end.

Project Components

Logo & Icon Design, Website Design & Development, UI
